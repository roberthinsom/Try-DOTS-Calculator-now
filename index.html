How the DOTS Calculator Works

The DOTS Calculator works by considering two key factors:

  1. The total lifted: This is the sum of the athlete’s best squat, bench press, and deadlift totals.
  2. Body weight: The athlete's weight class is factored in to calculate how much weight they are lifting relative to their body size.

The DOTS formula uses statistical models to adjust an athlete’s total, ensuring that the score reflects their performance in relation to their body weight. This is why trying the DOTS Calculator now is an excellent way to measure your lifting achievements on a fairer and more comparable scale.
